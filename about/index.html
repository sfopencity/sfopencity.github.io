About

To fill the blog, I hope to bring you film reviews, thoughts on the wider industry, and cover the year-round festivals that bond specialty film professionals around the bay area to let you in on the thriving and expanding world of film.

There is so much to learn and I would love to share my knowledge and training with you.

Thank you for taking the time to enjoy film as it surrounds this open city by the bay with me!
